About Me

I am a data scientist with a software-engineering backbone and proven experience delivering end-to-end solutions - from data wrangling and feature engineering to modeling, evaluation, and production deployment.

In prior roles, I modernized enterprise platforms in regulated settings - migrating legacy systems to a modern Python/Django services architecture, designing secure APIs for core transactional workflows, executing large-scale data migrations with minimal downtime, and strengthening role-based authentication, encryption, and auditability. I also delivered operational data applications on Linux/cloud infrastructure and designed data pipelines that supported analytics for performance, risk, and resource planning. Across these projects, I introduced CI/CD and observability and shipped dashboards that turned operations into decision-ready insights while laying a foundation for ML.

To deepen my analytics toolkit, I completed Google’s Data Analytics and Advanced Data Analytics Professional Certificates (~160 hours) with real-time machine learning implementation, and I also earned Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals (AI-900) to ground cloud-AI concepts.

Besides studies, I take part in various AI communities and have mentored Python learners and contributed to open source because teaching, sharing, and learning keep me sharp and collaborative.
